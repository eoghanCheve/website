    L3 Research internship (may - july 2024)

    Distance-based amino acid conservation score

    Équipe Dyliss - IRISA/Inria de l'Université de Rennes

    Details

    For this bioinformatics internship in the Dyliss team, I was supervised by François Coste and Pablo Espana Gutierrez.

    My goal was to find a cost function that takes into account evolutionary distances, enabling sequences to be weighted in a predictive model such as a PSSM or a HMM.

    In addition to achieving my goal,I created a predictive model that directly takes into account evolutionary distances and selection pressures .
